# AACC: Attribution for AI-Enhanced Creative Content

**A transparency framework for creators across all disciplines.**

## What is AACC?

AACC provides a simple, three-tier system for attributing AI use in creative work across writing, visual art, music, film, and more.

### The Three Tiers:

**1. AI-Modified (AI-Edited)**
- The creator produces the original content
- AI modifies or edits technical aspects, language, or structure for improved clarity, organization, or presentation
- **Attribution:** `By [Creator Name], AI-modified by [AI Name] or AI-edited by [AI Name]`
- **Example:** *"Mercury." By Sarah Chen, AI-Edited by Claude AI (word choice and line break suggestions)*

**2. AI-Assisted**
- The creator and AI collaborate throughout much of the creative process
- Though creator-driven, AI generates substantial elements that the creator selects, significantly modifies, or integrates into their vision
- **Attribution:** `By [Creator Name], AI-assisted by [AI Name]`
- **Example:** *Venus.* Dir. by Chris Taylor, AI-assisted by Runway ML (visual effects and scene generation)

**3. AI-Generated**
- AI creates the primary content from the creator's prompts and direction
- The creator's role is conceptual, curatorial, and editorial
- **Attribution:** `AI-generated by [AI Name], concept by [Creator Name]`
- **Example:** *"Mars." AI-generated by Wondera, concept by Sofia Martinez (Martinez specified genre and mood; AI generated track; Martinez added transitions)*

---

## Why AACC?

- **Simple:** Three clear tiers, easy to understand
- **Universal:** Works across all creative disciplines
- **Practical:** Designed for real-world attribution (gallery walls, book covers, credits)
- **Open:** Free to use and adapt under [Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 (CC BY 4.0)](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/)

---

## How to Use

1. Review the three tiers
2. Choose the tier that matches your AI involvement
3. Use the attribution format for your tier
4. See [detailed examples](https://davemalone.net/aacc/examples) for your discipline

---

## Use Examples by Tier

**Tier 1 (AI-Modified/Edited):**
- Writer edits with AI tools
- Filmmaker grades color with AI
- Musician masters audio with AI

**Tier 2 (AI-Assisted):**
- Visual artist paints over AI-generated backgrounds
- Writer substantially revises AI-drafted passages
- Photographer composites AI-generated elements

**Tier 3 (AI-Generated):**
- AI creates full image from text prompt
- AI composes song from style parameters
- AI generates video from scene descriptions
